About ME!!!

image of me.

Hello. I'm Tim. I like web design. I like games (all sorts). I like mobile development. This is my area of the interent where I try to create one, some, or many things combining one, some, or all the things I like.

So alittle about me.

I didn't start in tech. I dabbled in it growing up, learning (or rather understanding) enough to pimp out my Myspace page and figure out why certain lines of code work certain ways in on my Blogger account. And though my professional background lead me away from web design and development, I never lost interest in it. It wasn't until 2019 that I decided to make a shift and pursue a career I was passionate about. From then on, I did whatever I could to learn about web development and software engineering while maintaining a full-time career. It wasn't until Spring of 2023 where I pivoted again, shifting my focus and ability to learning what it takes to be a frontend developer and mobile app creator.

My 14 years of professional history is rooted in mentorship and coaching, crisis management, needs assessment, empathy and understanding in conflict resolution, and service excellence.

More than anything, let it be known that I'm a man quick with a story mixed with curious critical thinking and creative pursuit.
